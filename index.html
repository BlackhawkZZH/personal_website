About

I am a full-time Research Assistant at ai{DEAL} Studio at the Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech), under the supervision of Prof. Pengcheng An. My research focuses on human-IoT interaction and human-AI interaction for learning, education, accessibility, and general well-being.

I have expertise in developing interactive systems using multimodal sensing, large language models, and tangible interfaces. I am open to interdisciplinary collaborations.
